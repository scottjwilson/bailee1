What's the bailee app?

The Bailee App stands for ‘’The Black American & International Listings of Eager Entrepreneurs’’ — A global network of black owned businesses. We’ve designed the ultimate app that helps black owned businesses: Grow, build rapports with customers and stay connected with the people that matter the most! Our app helps consumers locate amazing black owned restaurants, inspiring life coaches or engaging personal trainers. We want people to be able to find all of these and more on our app. Our mission is to help black owned businesses thrive.
