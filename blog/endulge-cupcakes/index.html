Endulge Cupcakes

May 11th, 2017

What is the Endulge Cupcake Boutique?

Endulge Cupcake Boutique is a black owned cupcake shop located off Moreland Avenue in Atlanta, Ga.

What do they do?

When baking, they use all high quality, natural ingredients; including organic products when possible. They’re committed to baking daily in small batches and their cupcakes are fashionably scrumptious!!

What else should I know?

Endulge Cupcake Boutique stays connected to the community by offering internships through local culinary schools, programs at Le Cordon Bleu and the culinary arts program at Atlanta Technical College. If you’re ever in the area, you should definitely stop by and show your support!
